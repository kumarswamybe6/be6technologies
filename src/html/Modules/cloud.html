About the Course

What is Cloud Computing?

Cloud Computing is the delivery of computing services — servers, storage, databases, networking, software — over the internet. Every major enterprise is migrating to the cloud, creating an enormous demand for skilled cloud professionals. This program gives you hands-on experience with AWS, Azure, and GCP — the three platforms that power over 65% of the global cloud market.

◆Cloud is the backbone of every modern enterprise IT system
◆Global cloud market projected to exceed $1 trillion by 2028
◆Cloud roles are among the highest-paid in all of IT
◆Certified cloud engineers are in massive shortage globally
◆Skills apply across industries — from startups to government
